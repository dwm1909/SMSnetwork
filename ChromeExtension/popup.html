Questions

What is credit

Do you want to send more SMS, MMS and HLR lookup so you can accumulate credits through to accept the offers. If you already sent a text message one day a credit will be deducted automatically for the second SMS. If you have no credit so you can not send more than six messages per day.

What does "No status received" in my history?

"No state received" means that we have not received notice from our supplier if the SMS arrived. A common reason is that the recipient,s mobile phone is currently turned off.

How long does it take to get their credit after having exhausted an offer?

Everything is done automatically some deals you get on the fly, some take a while for companies to send them into the report so it can take up to 10 minutes on some before you get them.
